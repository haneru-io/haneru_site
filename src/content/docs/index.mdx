## Why Haneru?

Haneru provides clear, practical documentation for building and managing Shopify stores. Whether you're just starting out or looking to add advanced features, our guides help you navigate Shopify development with confidence.

**What you'll find here:**
- Beginner-friendly tutorials that explain concepts step-by-step
- Real code examples you can use in your projects
- Best practices for theme development and customization
- Analytics guides to help you understand your store's performance
