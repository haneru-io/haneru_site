## Why Haneru?

Haneru provides practical, no-fluff documentation for building and managing Shopify stores. The goal isn't to replicate what the official docs already cover well — it's to fill in the gaps, explain the gotchas, and walk through the things you only learn by doing.

**What you'll find here:**

- Beginner-friendly guides that explain the *why*, not just the *how*
- Real examples from an actual dev store, with screenshots
- Honest advice on what to prioritise (and what to skip)
- Practical coverage of ShopifyQL, Liquid, and the Shopify API
